Tips & Drills

Personalized tips and drills to improve your serve technique.

Forehand

Tip 1

Maintain a relaxed grip

A loose grip allows for greater racket head speed and flexibility in your swing. Avoid gripping the racket too tightly.

Drill 1

Shadow Swings

Practice your forehand swing without hitting a ball to focus on form and technique. Repeat 10 times.

Backhand

Tip 2

Use a two-handed backhand for stability

A two-handed backhand provides more control and power, especially for beginners. Keep your hands close together on the grip.

Drill 2

Wall Practice

Hit backhands against a wall to improve consistency and timing. Focus on maintaining a smooth swing.

Footwork

Tip 3

Stay on your toes

Maintain a balanced stance with your weight slightly forward on your toes to move quickly and efficiently.

Drill 3

Cone Drills

Set up cones and practice moving between them to improve agility and court coverage. Repeat 5 times.
